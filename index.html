About Me

I was born in Vietnam and was raised in the Silicon Valley. Growing up around new and evolving technologies, I quickly learned that I love it. I have a true passion for building new and visually pleasing applications. I am passionate about learning new technologies and excited to be able to learn something new in the industry everyday. Coming from the Asset Protection and Customer Success fields, I was eager to dive into Web Development.


To accelerate my learning, I attended Coding Dojo at the Silicon Valley location. Coding Dojo offers an extensive full-stack coding bootcamp that specifically covers all of the industry's most relevant and critical technologies: Python, MEAN Stack, and Java. Dojo instructors not only teach students how to write code, but they also engage with students to explain important concepts, transforming students into true web developers with a comprehensive set of tools that are crucial for success in their career as a software developer.
